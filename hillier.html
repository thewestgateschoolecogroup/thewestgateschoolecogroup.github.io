Stages
1. Collecting Milk Bottle Caps

Eco Group collects HDPE 2 bottle caps to use as raw material.

2. Cleaning

Then milk bottle caps are cleaned.

3. Weighing

Next we weigh the bottle caps and sort them into 300g food bags to get a consistent thickness.

4. Arranging Bottle Caps

Then Eco Group arranges the bottle caps on the sandwich press.

5. Melting

After being spaced out evenly, the bottle caps are melted.

6. Adding a Layer

Once the first layer has been melted, another layer of bottle caps is added and melted.

7. Pressing

Plastic sheets are compressed overnight.

8. Plastic Sheet

A small recycled plastic sheet is ready to be cut.

9. Cutting

Now a letter is cut out of the sheet.

10. Sanding

The letters are sanded to ensure smooth edges

11. All Letters Cut!

After the mammoth job of forming plastic sheets we have cut out SOLUTION NOT POLLUTION

12. Stakes Attached

Now the stakes are attached the letters are ready to be put up at Hillier's

13. Letters Planted

The letters have now been hammered into the ground at Hillier's climate unity project
